# 215. 民俗恐怖仪式与禁忌

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "design_folk_horror_ritual",
  "village": "Ghost Hollow",
  "taboo": "Do not look at the bride's face / Do not eat the red rice",
  "ritual": "The Midnight Wedding",
  "consequence": "Breaking the taboo turns you into a paper doll",
  "visuals": "Red lanterns, paper money, silent procession"
}
```

## 使用场景
灵异/悬疑文。构建中式民俗恐怖氛围。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **规则怪谈**：用看似荒谬但必须遵守的规则（禁忌）制造压抑感。
2.  **色彩反差**：大红（喜庆）与惨白（死亡）的视觉冲击。

}
