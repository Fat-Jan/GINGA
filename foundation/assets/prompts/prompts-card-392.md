# 392. 流派：克隆人/替身文学 (Clone Identity)

## 提示词内容

```json
{
  "task": "write_clone_identity_crisis",
  "trigger": "Finding a room full of bodies that look like MC",
  "dialogue": "Original: 'You are just a spare part.'",
  "clone_thought": "I have memories, therefore I am real.",
  "conflict": "Kill the original to become the only one",
  "twist": "MC realizes he is actually the original, or vice versa"
}
```

## 使用场景
科幻/悬疑/虐文。探讨“我是谁”的哲学命题。

## 最佳实践要点
1.  **存在主义**：克隆人对自我存在的质疑。
2.  **记忆**：记忆的真假是判断身份的关键。

## 示例输入
主角发现自己只是富豪为了续命培养的克隆体。
